WHAT IS VERMS?

VERMS means Visitor & Exhibitor Registration Management Software. VERMS is designed for Offices, Events, Libraries, Hotels, Societies and Museums. VERMS not only counts the number of visitors visiting on place, but also helps the security purpose and also analyze the trends. For example, how many time visitor spends on the place, how many times a visitor revisit on the place, purpose of visiting, meeting with staff and so on. Based on the report maintained, authorities can distribute their work among staff.

VERMS protect your facilities and employees. Minimize the risk of interloper and workplace violence. VERMS can carry out registration and counting of visitors. The information get stored in the database. The software is designed in such a way that it can create alerts about visitors who is going to visit. If the authorities feel that the person should not be given entry they can notify it to the security officials. With VERMS both employees and visitants can check-in safely.

BADGE DEMO

VERMS automatically prints badges when visitor check-in. You can also preprint the badges of your guest which saves time and help to minimize your guest precious hours. There are some demo of our predesigned badges which have QR code and barcode functionallity. Even you can scan QR code and Barcode in these demo. You can also customize your own badges according to your requirements.
